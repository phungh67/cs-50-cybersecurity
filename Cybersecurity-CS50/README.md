## 📖 Content Summary

The notes in this directory break down the core pillars of cybersecurity covered in the CS50 curriculum. Topics generally include:

* **Risk & Threat Modeling:** Identifying vulnerabilities, assessing impact, and understanding the attacker's mindset.
* **Authentication & Authorization:** Password policies, Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA), biometric security, and access control models.
* **Cryptography:** The principles of keeping data secret, including hashing, symmetric vs. asymmetric encryption, and digital signatures.
* **Malware:** Understanding the mechanics of viruses, ransomware, trojans, worms, and how they propagate.
* **Web & Network Security:** Concepts surrounding secure communication, firewalls, VPNs, and common web vulnerabilities (like SQL Injection and Cross-Site Scripting).
* **Social Engineering:** The human element of cybersecurity, including phishing, pretexting, and physical security.
